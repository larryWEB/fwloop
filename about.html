I KNOW NO FEAR WHEN I HAVE A CAMERA IN MY HANDS

Adigwe Ndubuisi is an illustrious and versatile professional photographer who runs and operates Fruithloopworks photography, a leading photography brand in Lagos, Nigeria. Ndubuisi specializes in documentary and portrait photography, using the critical elements of light, shadow, texture, share and lines to produce outstanding pictures of people, things and events. Inspired by a deep passion for arts and imagery, Ndubuisi enjoys telling timeless stories using photography. His excellent portfolio has afforded him the opportunity to travel across Nigeria, Africa and countries around the globe to execute photography projects. Ndubuisi has worked with several prominent brands across various sectors, including Access Bank Plc, Persianas Retail, Italian International School, Tribute Real Estate, Genevieve Nigeria, Ace Couture, Becca Apparel and Princess Ford Collections, to mention but a few.
